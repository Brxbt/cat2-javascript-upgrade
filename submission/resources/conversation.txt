/----WHO HE IS ----/
Mr. Anthony Maina is the founder and director of Santa Ana Calm Waters Academy,
  a CBE (Competency Based Education) school he established in 2017. The school
  serves students from Play Group (age 3) through Grade 9 (Junior Secondary).
  It is co-led by the school manager, Madam Christine(his wife).


  /----WHAT THE SCHOOL DOES----/
  Santa Ana provides holistic, Christian-values-based education combining academic
  excellence with character development. Subjects include Mathematics, English,
  Kiswahili, Science, Social Studies, Creative Arts, Computer Studies, French,
  Home Science, and Pre-Technical Studies. The school follows Kenya's CBE curriculum.
